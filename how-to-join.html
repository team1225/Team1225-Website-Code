Joining the Gorillas is easy!
If you need help anywhere throught the process, feel free to reach out. Contact Us

Step 1 | Fill out the new member form

Team 1225 requires all members to complete a short form that gathers contact information for the student and the parent.

Visit the link below to fill out the form, then continue to step 2

go.team1225.com/register

Link will open in a new tab

Step 2 | Membership with Boys and Girls Club

Team 1225 members are also required to fill out a membership form for the Boys and Girls Club of Henderson County. Membership with the Boys and Girls Club is required because they provide our much needed shop space.

  1. Complete Membership Application HERE
  2. Bring $5 Membership Card Fee to the Front Office to obtain Club Card*
  3. Renew Membership Annually

Step 3 | FIRST® Consent & Release

All members of the FIRST community must complete a Consent and Release agreement to compete in any FIRST Robotics Competition.

Note! This process can be tricky, if you need assistance Contact Us

  • Parent, go to firstinspires.org
    1. Click "Register" in the top right corner**
    2. After registering, go to your dashboard
    3. Click the "Parent/Guardian-Youth" Tab
    4. Click "Add Youth"
    5. Complete Consent and Release Forms
  • Student, go to firstinspires.org
    1. Click "Register" in the top right corner
    2. After registering, go to your dashboard
    3. Search for and join Team 1225
    4. Complete Consent and Release Forms

Step 4 | Join a Meeting

If you have completed Step 1, then you are ready to join us at a meeting!
Team 1225 is now meeting regularly, Mondays from 5:30 - 7:30 pm at the Boys and Girls Club of Henderson County.
1304 Ashe St, Hendersonville, NC 28792

Get directions
Important Additional Notes

Boys and Girls Club applications are accepted at the front office between the hours of 10am-2pm, Monday-Friday

Register yourself on this page for a new FIRST account, not your child. Enter your name, email address, and birthdate, not your child's. You will be able to add your child and apply to a team under the Parent/Guardian - Youth tab on your dashboard after your account has been created. Also, Students and Parents must have different emails In order for accounts to link together properly, the differing emails must match on one another's accounts.
