Ways to Donate

For your convenience, we have many ways you can donate to our team.
If you require assistance in the donation process, please contact us at funds@team1225.com, and we will gladly assist you.

4 Ways to Donate
  • PayPal
  • Boys And Girls Club
  • FIRST NC
  • Non Monetary

Donate Via PayPal

Making a donation through PayPal is easy! Just click the button and enter your amount.


Donate Via The Boys and Girls Club of Henderson County

To make any size tax-deductible contribution:

  1. Make a check to "Boys and Girls Club of Henderson County"
  2. Ensure "Robotics Team 1225" is in the memo line
  3. Mail the check to: Boys & Girls Club of Henderson County. PO Box 1460 Hendersonville, NC 28793

Donate Via FIRST® North Carolina

To make any size tax-deductible contribution:

  1. Navigate to the link HERE
  2. Click the blue "Donate Today" button
  3. Fill out the form on the opened page, and desginate "Team 1225, The Gorillas" in the comments box under the Additional Information section.

Donate Non Monetarily

Team 1225 always gladly accepts non monetary donation such as goods or services.
To donate any goods or services please reach out to us via funds@team1225.com and we will gladly assist you.
All donations are tax-deductible
